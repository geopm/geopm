GEOPM Bug Status




Note on Legends:

The age and cumulative plot legends show each bug as the combination of the three bug labels applied to it. These labels are three pairs of two letters separated by a dash. The first of each pair describes the category: 'q' for quality, 'p' for priority and 'e' for exposure. The second of each pair describes the bug having a high or low indicator for the category: 'h' or 'l'. For example the label 'qh-pl-el' describes a bug with high quality, low priority and low exposure.
