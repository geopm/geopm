Signals and Controls
====================

The signals and controls available to be read from the service may
vary depending on several factors including: the hardware installed on
the system, the device drivers that are enabled, the configuration of
kernel modules and other operating system settings.  The information
about the signals and controls available on a few specific GEOPM
enabled systems are given below.
